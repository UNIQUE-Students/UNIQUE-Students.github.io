[UNIQUE (Unifying Neuroscience and Artificial Intelligence in Qu√©bec)](https://sites.google.com/view/unique-neuro-ai) is a research centre working at the intersection of neuroscience and artificial intelligence research. It consists of more than 80 researchers affiliated with several universities and research centres from different regions of the province, and even from outside of it. The main goal of UNIQUE is to promote and facilitate exchanges between research teams working at the intersection of artificial intelligence (AI) and neuroscience.
